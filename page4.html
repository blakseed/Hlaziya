Indoor Access Flooring

The access flooring that comes with any finish on top

The indoor access flooring can come with any finish already applied onto the surface, such as Laminates, rubber, linoleum, parquet, stone materials, porcelain, LooseLAY, PVC or have Fitted carpets over the installed floor. This means that the underneath of the floors remains accessible regardless of the type of finish on the surface

Outdoor Access Flooring

The tough access flooring for the outdoors

The outdoor access flooring is tough, durable and extends the lifetime of waterproofing underneath it. It can also be installed over external services to increase useable space around the building or on roofs. Perfect for balconies and external spaces of relaxation. Can come with any finish you desire, including porcelain and stone materials

Server/Patch Rooms

The access flooring that offers a lifetime guarantee

Trusted by globally recognised companies, this floor aligns and levels perfectly. Unlike other floors in the market, it does not wobble when stepped on. The floor is tough but not as heavy, making it easy for technicians to lift and close without compromising the quality of the floor

Beautiful finish - solid core

The Monotec is a monolithic panel with special hygrometric characteristics of high mechanical resistance. It can be used for virtually all outdoor and indoor environments. It consists of a structural core that is monolithic, homogeneous and made entirely of inert material, sintered at high temperature with the top finishes tile that remains odourless 
There is no risk of dimensional changes in the presence of water. This allows a great flexibility in the management of the site. The assembly between finishing and support is provided by the gluing of the two materials through a specific adhesive. Support is free of asbestos and any other toxic material. The tiles have a self-extinguishing material on all the heights with a thickness of about 25mm
